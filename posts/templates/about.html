What is this?

StraightOuttaZuccotti.org is a people-powered forum to discuss the Occupy Wall Street movement on an personal level. We aim to find out whatâ€™s important to the participants.

Why?

People outside OWS seem to spend a lot of time wondering about what people at OWS are thinking . We want to create a place for individuals at OWS to voice those thoughts, and for protesters and observers alike to discuss, debate and weigh in on these ideas.

How does this work?

We've placed an erasable whiteboard near the library at Occupy Wall Street, with a prompt that reads "What do you think?", some markers, a disposable camera, and an email address.

This site is a collection of images taken of responses on the whiteboard. Once posted here, anyone can comment and weigh in on those responses.
